## Design Context

### Users
Recruiters, potential EA clients, and technical collaborators landing on Tarun's portfolio. Viewed on desktop primarily, often evening/late-night context (dark theme appropriate). The job to be done: assess whether Tarun is credible, interesting, and worth reaching out to — within 30 seconds.

### Brand Personality
**measured · operational · deliberate**

Not a hacker. Not a creative. An executor who also builds. The interface should feel like a well-used Moleskine on a CEO's desk — dense, precise, no decoration for decoration's sake.

### Aesthetic Direction
Editorial dark. Newspaper grotesque headlines (Darker Grotesque 900wt). Serif body for reading (Literata). Warm copper accent (#c9956a / oklch(68% 0.110 55)) — not neon, not indigo. All neutrals tinted toward H65 for subconscious warmth. Max content width 800px — editorial column width, not a wide marketing page.

Anti-references: neon-on-dark AI portfolio template, purple gradient hero, glassmorphism cards everywhere.

### Design Principles
1. **Content is the decoration.** Every section earns its place through words, not visual noise.
2. **One column reads.** Navigation is the only wide element. Content stays at editorial width.
3. **Hierarchy through weight, not color.** Darker Grotesque at 900wt does the heavy lifting — color accent is used sparingly (max 10% rule).
4. **No cards unless truly necessary.** Writing and work are rows, not boxed cards. Capabilities are a borderless 3-col list.
5. **OKLCH throughout.** Perceptually uniform, all neutrals tinted H65 toward brand hue.
